Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB)

PM SHRI +2 Adarsh High School Chausa proudly follows the curriculum prescribed by the Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB). Our academic program is designed to provide a solid foundation for junior students (Classes 6-8), rigorous preparation for secondary students (Classes 9-10), and specialized stream-based learning for senior secondary students (Classes 11-12).

  • ✔ Holistic development based on state educational standards.
  • ✔ Regular assessments and mock board examinations.
  • ✔ Focus on Science, Arts, and Commerce streams in +2 level.
